MVP Classroom Guide

Everything you need to pick today’s Most Valuable Player.

1. Sign in & Add classes

  • Select Sign in with Google.
  • Open Settings to add classes and student lists.
  • Switch between classes using the dropdown in the top bar.

2. Pick Today’s MVP

  • Click the Trophy icon to preset a winner. When presenting on a big screen, use your phone to avoid showing the selection to students. On Main page, press 'q' to show a QR code that lets you access MVP from your phone
  • Press Start to shuffle names and reveal the MVP.
  • MVPs are tracked automatically in the Weekly Winners timeline.

3. Manage Weekly Winners

  • Review past MVPs using the timeline on the right.
  • Select an entry to remove it if needed.
  • Reset the list at the end of each semester with Reset Weekly Winners.
